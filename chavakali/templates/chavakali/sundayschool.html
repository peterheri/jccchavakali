Joy Celebration Centre Chavakali.


Sunday School Ministry.

Vision : To impact Children to become like Christ

Mission.


Mathew 19:14
Let the Little Children come to me, and do not hinder them,for the Kingdom of Heaven belongs to such as these

Teaching, Directing and Sharing Knowledge of Jesus Christ to Children for their Edification and Fulfillment,
to be servants of the Light of God through Jesus Christ.

We are Raising up the next Generation for Christ. Our Vision is to equip children to be well grounded in God's Word,
build a Godly Character, develop spiritual gifts and definitely accept
Jesus as their saviour

Responsibilities undertaken in this department.


Here at Joy Celebration Center we believe that God wants to give a full life to his children, and God desires to rise up a generation of Spirit Filled Children
who love Him and testify about the amazing things He has done.

Our Desire is to partner with parents in helping their children establish a solid Bibilical foundation upon which to build the lives of their Children.

We help our kids to :

K - Know God and His Word
I - Involve in Friendship and Fellowship
D - Develop Spiritual Gifts
S - Serve in the church and Community
