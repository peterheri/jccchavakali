Joy Celebration Centre Chavakali.


Couples Ministry.

We seek to meet the concerns of both partners while strengthening and stabilizing marriages, Enabling couples to go the distance to fulfill God’s purpose for their lives.

Mission


Prayer – Real intimacy starts with pouring out our hearts to God. We come together to take out time to pray for our marriages.

Fellowship - Our fellowship events are designed to strengthen the relationships between Couples at JCC - Agape Fellowship. We can learn from one another.

Workshops – For our workshops we invite and experienced speaker or counselor in to address major marital issues. These sessions provide us with practical tool that can help enhance and build stronger marriages.

Ministry Purpose


The goal of the JCC - Agape Fellowship Centre Married Couples Department is to help build Godly marriages by providing opportunities for improving communication and pursuing intimacy in marriage

Why Participate in this Ministry


Marriage is God’s idea and he has a purpose and plan for each marriage.
He calls us to become one and model the relation between Jesus and the church.
Our marriages are opportunities that God can use to win men, women, boys and girls to Christ.
