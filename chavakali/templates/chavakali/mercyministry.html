Mercy Ministry Vision

Mission

JCC Mercy Ministry is a Community Based Organization that was established in 2005 and registered by the Kenya Government under the ministry of Culture and Social Services with its main office in Chavakali town Vihiga County. Our main objective is to develop, support, promote, and enhance creative solutions to problems that impede social development in our society in order to ensure qualitative living.

Mercy Ministry Mission

Our Focus

Our activity in the region therefore focuses on small enterprise development, environmental programmes, provision of basic needs and guiding & counselling for the disadvantaged children as well as HIV/AIDS infected and affected persons.

We are the Light Of the World

OUR TARGET GROUPS IN THE COMMUNITY ARE :


1. Widows and widowers
2. Orphans as a result of HIV/ AIDS
3. People living with AIDS (PLWAs)
4. The jobless youth

THE TECHNICAL AREAS WE FOCUS ON ARE :


(i). Prevention of Mother to Child Transmission of HIV (PMTCT).

(ii). Voluntary counseling and Testing services(VCT).

(iii). Adult Care and Treatment.

THE I CARE PROGRAMME


JCC MERCY MINISTRY, with support from members of the JCC Agape Fellowship Church- Chavakali, initiated the ‘I care Programme’ in 2004. This programme collect food stuffs, clothes and other house wares from well wishers and distribute to widows and orphaned children in Sabatia district in Vihiga County. Every 25th day of December, the I care programme puts a smile on the faces of the widows, widowers and orphaned children The “I Care programme” is also paying school fees for some orphaned children. Apart from members of JCC Agape Fellowship church who are the main supporters of the programme, the programme has also received support from the Vihiga mayor’s office, the Vihiga District Commissioner’s office and Tridah enterprises.
