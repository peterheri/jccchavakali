PROGRAMS


Church Planting

Every year in conjunction with other Agape Fellowship Church Branches, JCC participates in planting at least two churches in remote places where churches have not existed before or where there's great need for a spiritual church. Last year churches were planted at Solio Ranch in Nyeri as well as Shinyalu in Kakamega County. This year a church has been planted at Kapcherop in Trans Nzoia County. Plans are underway to construct a church in Shikusa Prison, Kakamega.

Acts Of Mercy

The Church has also participated in acts of mercy through construction of homes for needy people. Last year, the church, through Bukulunya Fellowship, constructed a house for a member after it was discovered he was living in dilapidated conditions. Before that, through Junction Fellowship, the church had helped renovate a house of a needy widow near Chavakali Junction. Through the Mercy Ministry, the church has also donated food stuffs and clothing to the needy during Christmas day. The community is also welcomed for special Christmas parties at the church as a way of sharing the Gospel of Christ with love.

School Ministry

The church has embarked on expanded efforts of reaching high school students with the Gospel of truth. Since its inception, the Outreach Ministry has been reaching an average of one school per month and an average of two schools per month in first term and second term of the school program respectively. In 2019 alone, the ministry has managed to spread the gospel to eleven schools and more than 500 students have given their lives to Christ.

Prison Ministry

The church has also embarked on spreading the good news in the prison. This is in a bid to ensure that even the prisoners get to hear the Good News. Plans are underway to undertake a Weekend Challenge in one of the prisons in the course of this year.

MEGA crusades & Door to Door evangelism

The church has carried out two MEGA crusades that have seen the gospel being preached concurrently through crusades and promotional vehicles. This has had a major impact in Vihiga County and beyond. Door to door evangelism has helped to bring the Gospel at the door steps of the people who may never bother looking for a place of worship.
