Current Projects.

In 2015, by the Help of the Almighty God we were able to get a plot in Chavakali. God helped us and we bought the land and we are now still in the process of constructing our new sanctuary, the church will hold a capacity of 5,000 people.

Currently we have just put windows and we are planning to put tiles on the floor. We are also trusting God for a good sound system. God has been forever faithful, believers in our church have taken a step of faith and took loans to help in constructing the House of God.

Your prayers and unwavering support has helped us reach this far. Thank you for your support, you are always in our prayers. The work we do is no longer in vain.If If you like to continue giving towards the church construction. Kindly click on the Give button above and select Church Construction.
